Body copy

Body copy should be used to communicate detailed information to users.

Aenean vestibulum metus pellentesque elit faucibus, placerat gravida nisl consequat. Maecenas dignissim, mauris id feugiat fringilla, nibh arcu interdum turpis, eu ornare orci velit quis nisi. Donec pulvinar tempus urna eget placerat.

<p>Aenean vestibulum metus pellentesque elit faucibus, placerat gravida nisl consequat. Maecenas dignissim, mauris id feugiat fringilla, nibh arcu interdum turpis, eu ornare orci velit quis nisi. Donec pulvinar tempus urna eget placerat.</p>

Lead copy

Lead copy is useful for subheadings or to call attention to important high-level information.

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Etiam pellentesque mollis metus, eget suscipit metus.

<p class="lead">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit.  Etiam pellentesque mollis metus, eget suscipit metus.</p>

Font weights

Use these font weight classes to change font weights on the fly. Choose from light, regular, and semibold to override standard font weights

Light and breezy.

Just plain old regular.

Semibold is less intimidating than bold.

<p class="light">Light and breezy.</p>
<p class="regular">Just plain old regular.</p>
<p class="semibold">Semibold is less intimidating than bold.</p>

Text colors

These color classes allow you to easily change the color of textual elements. Apply them to a parent element to change the color of children text elements.

Here's some blue text.

Want red? We've got that too.

Blue-alt is probably my favorite.

Green is another word for success!

Yellow should only be used on a dark background.

Works for icons, too! 

<p class="blue">Here's some <b>blue</b> text.</p>
<p class="red">Want <b>red</b>?  We've got that too.</p>
<p class="blue-alt"><b>Blue-alt</b> is probably my favorite.</p>
<p class="green"><b>Green</b> is another word for success!</p>
<p class="yellow"><b>Yellow</b> should only be used on a dark background.</p>
<p>
    Works for icons, too!
    <i class="icon icon-success green"></i>
    <i class="icon icon-info blue-alt"></i>    
    <i class="icon icon-caution yellow"></i>
    <i class="icon icon-close red"></i>
</p>
